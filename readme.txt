The Simple Crypto Currency Rate App is a user-friendly mobile application that allows you to monitor real-time cryptocurrency exchange rates. With a straightforward interface and a range of features, it's designed to provide cryptocurrency enthusiasts and investors with up-to-date information on their favorite digital assets.

## Project Overview

- **Real-time Data Display**: Get instant access to the latest cryptocurrency exchange rates.

- **Currency Conversion**: Choose from various fiat currencies to see rates in your preferred currency.

- **Cryptocurrency Details**: Dive deeper into each cryptocurrency with additional information such as market cap, trading volume, and historical price charts.

- **Auto-Refresh**: Enjoy peace of mind with automatic rate updates at regular intervals.

- **Search Functionality**: Find specific cryptocurrencies quickly using the built-in search feature.

## Features

### Home Screen

The home screen displays a list of selected cryptocurrencies and their current exchange rates in your chosen fiat currency. It provides a quick overview of the cryptocurrency market.

### Currency Selection

You can easily switch between different fiat currencies by selecting your preferred currency from the dropdown menu. The app will instantly update the displayed rates.

### Cryptocurrency Details

Access additional information for each cryptocurrency by tapping on its listing. Explore market capitalization, trading volume, and historical price charts to make informed decisions.

### Real-Time Updates

The app ensures that you always have the most up-to-date information by automatically refreshing exchange rates at regular intervals, typically every 30 seconds.

### Search Functionality

Use the search bar to find specific cryptocurrencies quickly. Just type the name or symbol, and the app will provide instant results.
